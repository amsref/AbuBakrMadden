About Me

Background

I am a social and cultural historian to heart and have specialised in Black and African pre and post Islamic history of the Moors (Blacks) and their scientific development in Northwest Africa and Andalusia in which Europe became highly developed. My expertise is in Black and African people’s behaviour, experiences and treatment as a minority group around the world.

Projects

Higher Educational Lecturer, Consultant and Public Speaker. I am currently working with the Welsh Assembly Government about the future of Black people in the 21st century as well as black African-Caribbean boys in the public schooling system in England and Wales.

Curriculum Development

Currently involved with the Welsh Assembly Government working group on developing a more inclusive curriculum andadvice to the teaching of themes related to Black, Asian and Minority Ethnic communities and experiences within the school curriculum.
